Mocha

Mocha is a simple Wear OS app for an important job — making sure that your coffee is brewed right every time.

Simply prepare your coffee, then choose the brewing method (such as cafetière, French press, espresso, etc.) in Mocha and tap the start button. Mocha will alert you with an alarm and/or vibration when your coffee is ready.

Customise Mocha by changing the brewing times for the pre-supplied methods, or even add your own.

Never suffer under- or over-brewed coffee again. Let Mocha tell you when.
